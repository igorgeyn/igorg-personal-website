Background

Before the PhD, I spent several years in Washington, D.C. working at the intersection of data and media. At Bloomberg Government, I built customer data pipelines feeding BGOV and Bloomberg Terminal products. At Atlantic Media, I was the primary analytical contact across marketing, editorial, and business teams—owning Leading Brands in Government, a flagship study that drove significant client revenue.

Earlier, I conducted health workforce research at UCSF, forecasting physician supply and demand across California. I've also done freelance data journalism (SPJ award winner), survey consulting, and various other projects that taught me how to translate complex analysis into clear, actionable insights.
